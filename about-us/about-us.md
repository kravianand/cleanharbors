# About Clean Harbors

Clean Harbors delivers environmental and industrial services that help customers operate safely, stay compliant, and protect the planet. Our teams partner with organizations in energy, manufacturing, and government to solve complex challenges with resilient solutions.

## Our Heritage

Founded more than four decades ago, Clean Harbors grew from a small regional service provider into a trusted global partner. We continue to invest in innovation, safety, and sustainability so we can support customers through every stage of their operations.

## Leadership You Can Trust

From emergency response to long-term maintenance, clients rely on Clean Harbors experts and industry-leading infrastructure. Our specialists provide rapid deployment, deep regulatory knowledge, and mission-critical support when it matters most.
